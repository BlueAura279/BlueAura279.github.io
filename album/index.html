About IST 250: Intro to Web Design

In PennState World Campus' IST 250 class, we covered the basics of what web design means. We covered the use of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in web design. We also covered the use of BootStraps, as well as hosting our websites on the internet through the use of a public web server.
