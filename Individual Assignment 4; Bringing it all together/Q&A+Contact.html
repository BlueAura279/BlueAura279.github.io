Commonly Asked Questions (Q&A)

Where should I look for a Gaming PC?

Overall, I would recommend going to a store (mostly Best Buy) during big sales and seeing what they have. If you would like to go the extra step and be prepared for what you want, I would recommend looking into what graphics card you want as well as how much storage (Ram & SSD) you want.

What PC brands should I look at?

I personally like ASUS, haven't had any problems with them and the software that comes with the PC to manage the PC I have really liked. IBUYPOWER and Cosair are good contenders as well!
